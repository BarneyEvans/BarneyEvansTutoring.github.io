How a typical lesson works

Steps

  1. Homework recap (5–10 mins)

    We quickly review key questions from last week, fix common errors and answer short questions.

  2. Live teaching (20–25 mins)

    New ideas are introduced with live coding, clear explanations and short checks for understanding.

  3. Guided exam practice (20–25 mins)

    Students solve exam-style Python questions at the right level, with support and feedback as they work.

  4. Wrap-up & homework (5 mins)

    We summarise the key idea, highlight common mistakes and set targeted homework to lock it in.

Close to exams, more lesson time is spent on past papers and timed questions.
